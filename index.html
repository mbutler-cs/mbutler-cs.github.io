Explore the Essence of China

19 Days - Departs Daily on a Private Basis

Beijing & Great Wall, Xian, Guilin, Hangzhou, Shanghai & Suzhou, Lijiang, Hong Kong

This is the ultimate luxury tour of China, visiting all of the country’s most popular locations and cultural highlights. Tour the cities of Beijing, Shanghai and Hong Kong.
